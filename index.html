Memoirs of a 24-year-old

A snake may bite you if you try to save it from a fire. That doesn't mean you should leave it in there.

But sometimes you will have to journey out in order to find a stick.

May you never stop saving snakes from the fire, but always remember to use a stick.

I hope you find your stick.
(p.s. its ok to give the snake some time to think about what it has done)
